Press Start Run, then use ← → and Space (or tap buttons) to keep the courier moving.

How to play

  • Land each jump on the next roof. Falling ends the run.
  • Grab floppy disks for score bursts and combo growth.
  • Avoid red antenna masts and broken rooftop edges.
  • The skyline scroll accelerates over time, hold your nerve.
