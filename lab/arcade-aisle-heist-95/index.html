Press Start Heist, then move with Arrow keys or WASD. Stay out of red patrol beams.

How to play

  • Collect every gold cartridge to clear the floor.
  • Patrol guards only see forward, but their beam reaches far.
  • Walls block vision, so use corners and timing to sneak through.
  • Getting spotted costs one life and restarts the current floor layout.
